Thermal Location Finder

How to Play

  • You will be given a target city to locate on a blind map (its name is hidden at first!).
  • Click the map to place your guess marker, then click Confirm Guess.
  • You have 3 attempts per location. Correct guess range is 1000 km.
  • Every 15 seconds, a new hint unlocks (first hint is available immediately):
    1. Famous Cultural/Historical Fact (At Start)
    2. Continent Name (At 15s)
    3. Neighboring Countries/Waters (At 30s)
    4. Thermal Mouse Pointer (At 45s)
  • Score depends on distance accuracy and response speed. Good luck!
